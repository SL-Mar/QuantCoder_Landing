QuantCoder_FS

Towards Automating Quantitative Finance Research

QuantCoder_FS is a full-stack AI-powered platform designed to automate the transformation of academic research into actionable trading strategies. It bridges the gap between complex theoretical finance and real-world quantitative implementation.

Key Features

  • PDF summarization using LLMs
  • YouTube transcript analysis for insight extraction
  • Agent-based article-to-code transformation
  • Fundamental and news analysis via EODHD
  • QuantConnect-compatible strategy generation
  • User-configurable AI workflows and persona prompts

Platform Architecture

QuantCoder_FS combines a FastAPI backend with a Next.js frontend. It integrates language models, financial APIs, and agentic workflows to support automated summarization, research parsing, and code generation.

Development Roadmap

  • Q2 2025 – Integration with QuantConnect's LEAN Engine
  • Q2 2025 – Fundamental alpha extraction (Piotroski F-Score, valuation models)
  • Q3 2025 – Cloud deployment for scalable access
  • Q4 2025 – Integration with Notion for research and strategy management
